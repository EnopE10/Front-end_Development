How Volunteer Overseas Works

1 Find Your Perfect Project

Research and compare the best projects abroad based on what matters to you:

  • Location
  • Activity
  • Duration
  • Start Dates
  • Where's your dream destination?
  • What do you see yourself doing?
  • How much time do you have?
  • Fixed and flexible start dates
  • (varies by program)

2 Apply Now

Follow our easy and secure application process:

  • 1 Fill out your trip preferences
  • 2 Tell your program why you are a good fit
  • 3 Reserve your spot with a small deposit

* This deposit is generally a small portion of the project fee. Your deposit secures your spot with the program and shows that you are a serious applicant. This deposit is fully refundable if you are not accepted by the program.

3 Wait to Hear Great News

After you submit your application, you will hear from your program within 3-7 business days.

Applying through Volunteer Overseas automatically qualifies you to:

  • $ Enter to win a scholarship from Volunteer Forever (up to $1,000)
  • $ Raise funds for your trip on Volunteer Forever's no-fee crowdfunding platform

Talk about great news!

4 Finalize your trip

After you've been accepted, your program will help you with the not so little details:

  • The remainder of your program fee
  • The remainder of your program fees
  • The remainder of your program
  • The remainder
  • And to...
